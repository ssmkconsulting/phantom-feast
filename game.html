Quick reflex arcade

Phantom Feast

Glide your core through the field, collect energy orbs, snag rare moving gold boosts, dodge spectral ghosts, and stack your multiplier before the clock runs out.

  • Move with your mouse or touch. The core eases toward your pointer.
  • Collect teal orbs to add time and build multiplier; gold boosts are worth more but vanish fast. Triple orbs give 10s of 3× points.
  • Avoid poison dots—they drain points, summon ghosts, and make your core go queasy. Micro dots are tiny but supersize you briefly.
  • Ghosts end the run on contact—bait and sidestep their turns.
  • After a high score, spike obstacles appear—touching them ends your run instantly. Super ghosts may chase; stun them with the special stop orb.
  • Lights might flicker off for a second—stay calm and keep moving.

Pro tips

Ride the edges to control space, chain orbs for 5x, and give yourself room before hazards turn.
